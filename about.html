About Me

Hi, I'm Huanyi (Lydia). I am a MFADT student at Parsons, currently taking Creative Coding Lab. I like working between digital and physical media, especially projects that combine code, visuals, and small objects that make a desk or room feel more alive.

My background includes interactive media, basic web design, and experiments with motion and simple game-like interactions. In this class, I focused a lot on using p5.js to explore themes.

Education

I am studying at Parsons School of Design (The New School) in the MFA Design and Technology program. Creative Coding Lab is one of the courses where I started to connect programming with visual storytelling and interaction.

Interests

Outside of class, I enjoy play games, photography, and movie. I like activities with groups since I am ENFP in 16 personalities which is love stay with friends.

Career Goals

In the future, I am interested in working in areas related to interaction design, creative technology, or digital installation work. I want to keep learning how to use code as a design material, and how to build experiences that feel both technically thoughtful and emotionally honest.

Thoughts on Technology

My relationship with technology is a mix of curiosity and caution. I like how tools like p5.js, TouchDesigner, and simple sensors can turn feelings or data into visuals, but I also think a lot about pace, overload, and how much of our lives are filtered through screens.
